Introduction

In this page you will learn how to make the most wonderful chilli, the best for your game days!

Let's get started. Keep up with our ingredients!

Ingredients

  • Vegetable Oil
  • 3 Onions and 1 head garlic
  • 1 yellow bell pepper and 1 orange bell pepper
  • Cubed beef stew meat and italian sausage meat
  • Applewood smoked bacon
  • Cans tomato sauce and and cans tomato paste
  • Cans black beans and cans kidney beans
  • Beef broth

Steps

  1. On medium-high heat stir the onions, garlic and bell peppers for about 5 minutes
  2. Add the beef stew meat, sausage and bacon amd cook until eavenly browned
  3. Stir in the tomato sauce, tomato paste, black beans, kidney beans, chipotle peppers with sauce, beef broth, beer, chili powder, oregano, pepper, salt, and chocolate.
  4. Bring the mixture to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat to low and simmer for at least 1 hour; 2 hours is preferred
