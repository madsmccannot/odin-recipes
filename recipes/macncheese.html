Introduction

In this page you will learn how to make a great portion of Mac' n Cheese!

Let's get started. Keep up with our ingredients!

Ingredients

  • Refrigerated Macaroni and Cheese
  • Shredded white and/or yellow sharp Cheddar Cheese
  • Bacon
  • Green Onions
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Panko bread crumbs
  • Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • Melted Butter and Kosher Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 190ÂºC.
  2. Add the macaroni, all the cheeses, bacon, green onions and cayenne pepper to a bols and stir until well combined
  3. Transfer mixture to a 2-quart baking dish
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes
  5. Meanwhile, prepare the topping: add panko, parmesan, butter and salt to a bowl and stir until well combined
  6. Top mac and cheese with breadcrumb topping and return to the oven. Bake until lightly browned and bubbly around the edges, about 15 minutes more.
