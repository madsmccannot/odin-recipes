Introduction

In this page you will learn how to make the most fantastic lasagna you will eat in all your life!

Let's get started. Keep up with our ingredients!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna pasta
  • Cheeses

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the pasta
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
